2021 Student Showcase

Ashley Giovannini

Instagram: @giovanniniphotography

Ashley is a wildlife photographer studying zoology. She has been practicing photography since she was 12 years old and aspires to one day be working as a wildlife/documentary photographer full time.

Most of her photos are taken out in the field of animals that she tracks. She also enjoys landscape photography.

Caption: Moody Mountain. Over looking the Vancouver Harbour across to North Vancouver the mountain range could be seen. This photo was taken just as the sun was finishing setting.

Caption: Little Squirrel. I saw this squirrel munching on peanuts that someone left in Stanley Park. It was so caught up in eating I was able to get close for a photo.

Caption: Mule Deer. This photograph was taken of a juvenile mule deer at Knox Mountain Park. It was a cloudy day causing a darker/moodier scene. Of course the deer saw me and was curious of my presence, explaining the intense gaze into the camera.
