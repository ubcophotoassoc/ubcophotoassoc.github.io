Jessica Craig

Instagram: @spotsandstripesphotography

Jessica is an amateur photographer from Calgary who specializes in wildlife and theme park photography. This year she graduates from UBCO with her BSc in Zoology.

She is completely self-taught and is always excited about learning new tips and tricks and trying out new styles. When she's not photographing animals, she can be found listening to Taylor Swift's music, attempting to write novels, and playing soccer.

Caption: This photo was taken at RZSS Edinburgh Zoo in the Fall of 2019. I had stopped by the koala enclosure just as the keeper's had woken them up for their weekly weigh checks.
